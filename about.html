I am a UX designer with 8 years’ experience in strategy and project development. I work full-time at WOCCU, where I am designing and managing our company’s transition to SharePoint, develop content for web and print, and manage & design development finance projects in emerging economies.

On the side, I am a freelance UX/UI designer and marketing consultant to small businesses and start-ups.

I found myself in design after watching a TedX Talk by David Butler, Coca-Cola’s first and former global head of design. You can watch it here. Then reading his book, which you can find here. He applied design principles on business operations to create a new system that made the large company operate as a start-up, always prototyping and innovating, and in the process saveed them over $500 million.

The potential of design - making things practical, usable, and beautiful - excited me.

So I started with designing and writing projects in resource-poor countries. Little by little, I’ve increased my skills in design software and Microsoft products. Now I also help small businesses with strategy, marketing, and user experience.

I love traveling, getting to know people, and learning new things. Most recently, I was in rural Philippines interviewing women entrepreneurs. And my next trip is Milan!
