g for short, rose for translation

I am a UX designer with 8 years’ experience in strategy and project development.

Currently, I work as a freelance UX/UI designer and marketing consultant to small businesses and start-ups.

I like to think BIG

I began my journey in design after being inspired by a TedX Talk by David Butler, Coca-Cola’s first and former global head of design. And again after reading his book where he explains how he applied design principles on business operations to create a new system that made the large company operate as a start-up, always prototyping and innovating, and in the process saved them over $500 million.

The potential of design - making things practical, usable, and beautiful - excited me.

So I started with designing projects in resource-poor countries, and little by little, I’ve increased my skills in design software and Microsoft products.

I enjoy discovering new perspectives

I love traveling, meeting people, and learning new things. Most recently, I was in rural Philippines interviewing women entrepreneurs to better understand how financial technology could make their lives easier.
