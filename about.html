My name is Gulbahar — call me G for short or Rose for translation.

Made in NY, studying in Milano.

what drives me

You know that feeling when you’re overcome with excitement and don’t want the conversation to end, so you keep picking at the food on the table and ordering more coffee, all because you’ve found someone who talks about the circular economy as excitedly as you do? These conversations continually convince me of the power of design and the need to think holistically.

what brought me here

As a new business designer within the social innovation sector, I’ve had the unique opportunity to design programs that try to solve challenging problems in resource-poor environments like affordable housing finance in Haiti and agricultural input centers in Bangladesh.

A couple of years ago, led by my interest in human-centered design, I wanted to become a UX designer. I enrolled in Bloc’s design bootcamp and am now proficient in HTML, CSS, Adobe Creative Cloud, Sketch, Balsamiq, and Figma. However, after designing and coding this portfolio, I realized that I really enjoy the business end of design. So now, I mix my design and business tools to guide my processes. For instance, I created a proposal design canvas - inspired by the Business Model Canvas - that I used to develop projects. Here is the case study where I use this tool.
