Strategist based in DC.

My name is Gulbahar Okuyan, but call me Rose. Born to a Turkistan-American family, I've always been surrounded by rich, competing cultures. Growing up with a mixture of nomadic ancestry with farmer and engineer grandparents and an architect-trained father and artistic mother, I enjoy facing new challenges and learning new things. I have lived in 3 countries and traveled to more than 25 and am constantly redecorating or moving apartments.

I'm currently the senior new business manager at IESC, a nonprofit consulting firm working on small business development. I’ve always gravitated towards challenging roles in resource-poor environments, whether it’s working on a small team or in social innovation. I started my career managing international monitoring & evaluation projects, then moved into business development roles, focusing on strategy and project design and development.

I have an MBA from SDA Bocconi and certifications/coursework in strategy, innovation, and design thinking.
