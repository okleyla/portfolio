Business designer & developer shuffling between NYC & DC.

My name is Gulbahar Okuyan, but call me Rose.

Born to a Turkistan-American family, I have always been surrounded by rich, competing cultures. I grew up in Brooklyn and NJ to a mixture of nomadic ancestry, with farmer and engineer grandparents, and an architect/ framer father and artistic mother. All their traits rubbed off on me. I have lived in 3 countries and traveled to more than 25. And am constantly redecorating or moving apartments. I enjoy change and trying new things.

Career-wise, I’ve always gravitated towards challenging roles in resource-poor environments, whether it’s a very small company/ team or in a developing country. I started my career managing international monitoring & evaluation projects then moved more into business development roles because I enjoyed designing and creating the projects. At each company transition, I would take on more responsibility, and thus gain more insight into company operations. This is when I started taking extra work in design ops and created manuals, tools, and forms for business development and internal operations.

To further develop my skills in my interests, I signed up for a design bootcamp then enrolled into an MBA program in Italy. Now, I’m open for freelance or full-time opportunities in business development, business design, and/or design ops.
